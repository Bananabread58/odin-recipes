Cakepop recipe

Ingredients

  • Premade Cake
  • Melting Wafers
  • Frosting
  • CakePop Sticks

Steps

  1. First off, crumble the cake and put it in a large bowl
  2. Then mix in a couple spoonfuls of frosting in the mixture
  3. Now scoop the mix and roll it into a ball
  4. Then place cake pop on the stick
  5. Then you can melt the melting wafers and dip the cakepops in it for a chocolate finish!
  6. Now you have cakepops!
