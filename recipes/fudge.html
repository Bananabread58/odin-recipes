Fudge recipe

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Chips
  • Sweeteend Condensed Milk
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. First off,combine the chocolate chips and sweetend condensed milk in a small sauce pan
  2. Then place the sauce pan on the stove on low heat.
  3. Stir consistently while the chocolate melts, once melted, make sure to stir in a couple of drops of vanilla extract.
  4. Stir until the chocolate is smooth and shiny
  5. Then pour it into a 8x8 inch pan, cover it, and let the fudge set at room temperature for 4 hours
  6. Now you have fudge! :D
